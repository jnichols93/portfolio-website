About the project...



My fourth buildweek at Lambda, was by far my favorite one! In this four day project I was in charge of building a fully functioning Database, as-well as a CRUD(Create Read Update Delete) API.

Unlike previous buildweeks, I was not working with a partner. Instead I had to produce my half of the project individually. It was a great experience collaborating with the other developers on my team, in order to produce the back-end counterparts to their front-end components!

Due to the fact that I had started working on my part of the project over the weekend, I was able to wrap up early and assist the Front-End developers with react related questions, and troubleshooting with them, which was an excellent learning opprotunity for me.

Heads up! I Know my environment variables are visible, they usually wouldnt be. However I wanted to show that I can use them.

Overview:

Food Truck Trackr, is an application every foodie needs! It gives users the ability to find their favorite food trucks around town, and where they plan to be next.
