Hey I'm Justin!



I am a full stack developer based out of Minneapolis, MN. I write in both Python and JavaScript, and I am familiar with multiple front and back end frameworks such as; React, Redux, Node.js, Express, Django and Flask. I live and breathe software development, on average I spend about 50 hours per week learning and writing code. I am driven by curiosity, and a passion for technology.

My career arc is a little unconventional, after high school, I joined the United States Marines. I served five years, four on active duty and one year in the active reserve. I served as an 0621 Field Radio Operator, or FRO for short. My job was to establish and maintain communications, between the forward element and headquarters. In short, I walked around with a radio on my back all of the time. However it was not nearly as bad as I've made it sound! My inner nerd flourished while in the Marines, I would teach classes on building improvised antennas. Once even using the base fencing to get a radio check from Hawaii, while in Japan! During this time I also served as the Personal Radio Operator for Third Marine Divisions, Commanding Officer. This position allowed me to get trained on pretty much anything, which is where I wrote my first lines of code while learning a mobile command center platform known as NOTM (Network On The Move.)

After deciding to enter civilian life again, I attended the University of Houston, where I studied business management and administration. During this time I worked as a Sales Consultant. Working for several different brands including Dodge, Ford, Toyota, and Mercedes-Benz. Eventually moving back to my home state of Minnesota, where I enrolled in Lambda School so I could follow my passion, and transition into a career in Software Development.
