ABOUT THE PROJECT


For My first Build Week project, I worked on Luncher, an App on a mission to address childhood hunger. Luncher does this, by serving as a crowd-funding platform for schools to reach out to their communities, for assistance feeding the kids who need it.

As a new student at Lambda, our first month was focused on HTML and CSS/LESS, with some JavaScript thrown in! So in order to show what I had learned, I focused on the marketing page.

As a precaution, Id like to state I had zero training in UI/UX design, and I had no UI/UX designer or design to work from, SO that being said This was the result!!
